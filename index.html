Juniper Mountain House.

Make your Rocky Mountain escape just 30 minutes from Denver.

Welcome to Juniper Mountain House, a boutique hotel and gathering space nestled on 10 acres of lush woodland, offering breathtaking views of the Evergreen, Colorado foothills. Immerse yourself in a world of all-season adventures, with private hiking trails on our property, as well as convenient access to mountain biking, water sports, skiing, hot springs and so much more.

Indulge in the epitome of relaxation amidst the mountains, where our on-site hot tub, outdoor fire pit, and inviting rocking chairs overlooking the mountains await you. Our thoughtfully curated hotel experiences, such as craft beer tastings and romance packages, are designed to enhance your stay and make it truly special.

Sneak away for a weekend among like-minded travelers and kindred spirits. With only five tastefully appointed bedrooms and inviting communal spaces, you’ll have the run of our scenic mountain retreat.
